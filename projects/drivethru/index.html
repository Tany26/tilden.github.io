Real-Time, Digestible Data

Drive-Thru Monitor is focused on conveying the information your employees need without overwhelming them with numerical statistics.

A simple bar graph compares the current average drive-thru service times with a pre-selected goal time, and bubbles placed near the cars quickly convey exactly how long each car has been waiting.

Your restaurant. Not someone else's.

Unlike other drive-thru monitoring systems on the market, which use highly abstract representations of road layouts, Drive-Thru Monitor uses visuals designed based on aerial views of actual drive-thrus.

Because of this, whether your restaurant has parallel order areas, a separate payment area, or even is located a country where people drive on the left, Drive-Thru Monitor supports layout.

Inviting Graphics

Drive-Thru Monitor features video-game inspired visuals, including animated car motion and a real-time day/night cycle. Watching the little cars drive around can be kind of fun, like watching a train set...
